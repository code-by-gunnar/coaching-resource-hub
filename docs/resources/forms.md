# Essential Coaching Forms & Templates

Comprehensive collection of forms and templates for managing your coaching practice, client relationships, and business operations. All forms are customizable and designed to enhance your professional coaching services.

### Client Intake Form
**Purpose:** Comprehensive client onboarding and background information gathering.
- Personal and professional background
- Current challenges and opportunities  
- Previous coaching or therapy experience
- Goals and expectations for coaching
- Communication preferences and scheduling needs
- Confidentiality agreement and consent forms

### Client Session Record
**Purpose:** Track session notes, progress, and action items.

- Breakthrough moments and challenges

### Client Feedback Form
**Purpose:** Gather structured feedback from clients to improve your coaching effectiveness.
- Post-session feedback collection
- Program evaluation and testimonials
- Areas for coach improvement
- Client satisfaction ratings

### Client Outreach Templates
**Purpose:** Professional communication templates for client outreach.
- Initial contact and inquiry responses
- Follow-up communications and check-ins
- Program invitations and opportunities
- Re-engagement and retention emails

## üéØ Assessment Forms

### Wheel of Life Assessment
**Purpose:** Visual tool to assess client satisfaction across life domains.

A "Wheel of Life" is a visual tool used in life coaching to assess and understand different aspects of a person's life, allowing them to identify areas where they might need to focus more energy to achieve greater balance and satisfaction across their personal and professional spheres.

**Life Domains Include:**
- Career & Professional Development
- Financial Wellbeing  
- Health & Fitness
- Relationships & Family
- Personal Growth & Learning
- Recreation & Fun
- Physical Environment
- Community & Contribution

**Online Tool:** [Wheel of Life Assessment](https://wheeloflife.noomii.com/)

### Values Clarification Assessment
**Purpose:** Help clients identify core values that guide decision-making.
- Values identification exercises
- Priority ranking worksheets
- Values-goals alignment checks
- Decision-making frameworks

### Strengths Assessment Forms
**Purpose:** Identify and leverage client strengths and talents.
- Strengths discovery questions
- Talent inventory worksheets
- Strength application exercises
- Development planning templates

### Coaching Feedback Template
**Purpose:** Structure for giving and receiving coaching feedback.

Coaching feedback helps people identify areas for improvement, and can motivate and promote self-awareness. It's a critical part of the coaching process.

- Feedback delivery frameworks
- Self-assessment questionnaires
- 360-degree feedback collection
- Progress review templates

## üéØ Using These Forms Effectively

### Implementation Best Practices
1. **Customize for Your Brand** - Add your logo, colors, and contact information
2. **Adapt to Your Process** - Modify fields to match your specific coaching approach  
3. **Digital Integration** - Use with your preferred coaching platform or CRM
4. **Client Privacy** - Ensure all forms comply with GDPR and local privacy regulations
5. **Regular Updates** - Review and refresh forms quarterly based on client feedback

### Form Introduction Strategy
- **Onboarding Phase** - Introduce essential forms during client intake
- **Explain Value** - Help clients understand how forms enhance their experience
- **Keep It Simple** - Start with core forms and add complexity gradually
- **Feedback Loop** - Ask clients about form effectiveness and adjust accordingly

### Digital vs. Physical Forms
**Digital Advantages:**
- Easy sharing and completion
- Automatic data organization
- Environmental sustainability
- Real-time collaboration

**Physical Advantages:**
- No technology barriers
- Personal, tactile experience
- Works in any environment
- Some clients prefer paper
