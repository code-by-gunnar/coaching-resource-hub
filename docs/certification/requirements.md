## üåé ICF (International Coaching Federation) Requirements

### **ACC (Associate Certified Coach)**
*Entry-level ICF credential*

#### **Training Requirements:**
- **60+ hours** of coach-specific education from ICF-accredited program
- Must cover ICF Core Competencies and Code of Ethics
- Training must be completed within 3 years of application

#### **Experience Requirements:**
- **100+ hours** of coaching experience with at least 8 clients
- Minimum 75 hours must be paid coaching
- 25 hours can be pro-bono coaching

#### **Mentor Coaching:**
- **10+ hours** with ICF-credentialed mentor coach (PCC or MCC level)
- Must focus on ICF Core Competencies development
- Can be individual or group sessions

#### **Assessment:**
- **Written examination** covering coaching knowledge and ICF competencies
- **Coach Knowledge Assessment (CKA)** - multiple choice format
- Passing score: 82% or higher

#### **Other Requirements:**
- Complete online application with detailed coaching log
- Provide 2 audio recordings of coaching sessions
- Submit signed Code of Ethics agreement
- Pay application and credential fees ($300-500)

### **PCC (Professional Certified Coach)**
*Mid-level ICF credential*

#### **Training Requirements:**
- **125+ hours** of coach-specific education from ICF-accredited program
- Must include advanced coaching skills and competencies
- Training must be completed within 7 years of application

#### **Experience Requirements:**
- **500+ hours** of coaching experience with at least 25 clients
- Minimum 450 hours must be paid coaching
- 50 hours can be pro-bono coaching

#### **Mentor Coaching:**
- **10+ hours** with ICF MCC-credentialed mentor coach
- Advanced competency development focus
- Individual sessions recommended

#### **Assessment:**
- **Written examination** (same as ACC but higher expectations)
- **Performance Evaluation** of recorded coaching sessions
- More rigorous competency demonstration required

### **MCC (Master Certified Coach)**
*Highest level ICF credential*

#### **Training Requirements:**
- **200+ hours** of coach-specific education
- Advanced leadership and mentoring components
- Continuing education throughout career

#### **Experience Requirements:**
- **2,500+ hours** of coaching experience
- Minimum 35 clients over coaching career
- Demonstrated coaching mastery and impact

#### **Assessment:**
- **Oral examination** with ICF assessors
- **Portfolio submission** with coaching artifacts
- **360-degree feedback** from clients and peers
- Most rigorous assessment in coaching industry

## üá¨üáß AC (Association of Coaching) Requirements

### **AC Practitioner**
*Entry-level AC credential*

#### **Training Requirements:**
- **100+ hours** of coaching education from AC-approved provider
- Must cover core coaching skills and ethical practice
- Emphasis on reflective practice and learning

#### **Experience Requirements:**
- **100+ hours** of coaching practice
- Variety of clients and coaching contexts
- Detailed practice log and reflection

#### **Mentor Coaching:**
- **35+ hours** of mentoring/supervision
- Focus on reflective practice and skill development
- Usually spread over 12+ months

#### **Assessment:**
- **Portfolio-based assessment**
- Reflective essays on coaching practice
- Evidence of continuous learning and development
- **No written exam required**

### **AC Member**
*Standard professional AC level*

#### **Training Requirements:**
- **200+ hours** of comprehensive coaching education
- Advanced coaching skills and methodologies
- Research-informed practice emphasis

#### **Experience Requirements:**
- **100+ hours** of supervised coaching practice
- Evidence of coaching effectiveness and impact
- Ongoing professional development

#### **Mentor Coaching:**
- **35+ hours** of professional mentoring
- Supervision of coaching practice
- Peer learning and development focus

#### **Assessment:**
- **Enhanced portfolio submission**
- Professional practice evidence
- Peer review and recommendation
- Commitment to ongoing development

## üìö Training Program Selection

### **ICF-Accredited Training Programs**

#### **What to Look For:**
- **ACTP (Accredited Coach Education Program)** - Full 125+ hour programs
- **ACSTH (Approved Coach Specific Education Hours)** - Individual courses
- **CCE (Continuing Coach Education)** - Ongoing development

#### **Program Types:**
- **Live virtual training** - Interactive online sessions
- **In-person intensives** - Weekend or week-long programs
- **Hybrid programs** - Combination of online and in-person
- **Self-paced online** - Flexible timing options

### **AC-Approved Training Programs**

#### **What to Look For:**
- **AC-approved providers** with research-based curricula
- **Reflective practice emphasis** throughout program
- **Peer learning opportunities** and group supervision
- **Evidence-based approaches** to coaching

## ‚è∞ Timeline Expectations

### **ICF Certification Timeline:**
- **ACC:** 6-18 months (depending on coaching hours accumulation)
- **PCC:** 1-3 years (500 hours takes time to accumulate)
- **MCC:** 5-10+ years (requires extensive experience)

### **AC Certification Timeline:**
- **Practitioner:** 12-24 months (includes mentoring period)
- **Member:** 18-36 months (enhanced portfolio development)

## üéØ Choosing Your Path

### **Consider ICF If:**
- You want globally recognized credentials
- You prefer structured, standardized requirements
- You plan to coach internationally
- Corporate coaching is your target market

### **Consider AC If:**
- You're based in UK/Europe
- You prefer reflective, portfolio-based assessment
- You value research-informed practice
- You want more flexible development pathways

## üîÑ Maintaining Your Certification

### **ICF Renewal Requirements:**
- **40 CCE hours** every 3 years
- **10 hours** of mentor coaching (for ACC/PCC)
- Annual membership fees
- Code of Ethics agreement renewal

### **AC Renewal Requirements:**
- **25 CPD hours** annually
- Ongoing supervision/mentoring
- Annual membership fees
- Professional development portfolio updates
