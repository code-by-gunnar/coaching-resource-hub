# Client Assessment Forms for UK Coaches

Professional assessment and intake forms designed specifically for UK coaching practices, ensuring compliance with UK data protection laws and professional standards.

## üéØ Essential Assessment Forms

### 1. Initial Client Consultation Form

**Purpose:** Gather essential information during discovery calls while ensuring GDPR compliance.

#### Key Sections to Include:

**Personal Information**
- Full name and preferred name
- Contact details (email, phone, address)
- Emergency contact information
- Preferred communication methods
- Time zone (for online coaching)

**Coaching Focus Areas**
- Primary reason for seeking coaching
- Current challenges and pain points
- Desired outcomes and goals
- Previous coaching or therapy experience
- Timeline expectations

**Practical Considerations**
- Availability for sessions
- Preferred session format (in-person/online)
- Budget range
- Payment preferences
- Any accessibility requirements

**Data Protection Notice** (UK-Specific)
```
I understand that my personal data will be processed in accordance 
with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. I have read and 
understood the privacy policy provided.

Signature: _________________ Date: _________________
```

### 2. Coaching Readiness Assessment

**Purpose:** Evaluate if a potential client is ready for coaching and if you're the right coach for them.

#### Readiness Indicators Scale (1-5)

Rate each statement from 1 (Strongly Disagree) to 5 (Strongly Agree):

- I am ready to make changes in my life
- I can commit time and energy to the coaching process
- I am open to exploring new perspectives
- I take responsibility for my actions and outcomes
- I am willing to be challenged and held accountable
- I can maintain confidentiality about other clients
- I understand coaching is different from therapy/counselling

**Red Flags to Watch For:**
- Expecting the coach to "fix" their problems
- Unable to commit to regular sessions
- Seeking therapy rather than coaching
- Unwilling to take action between sessions
- Unrealistic expectations about outcomes

### 3. Goal-Setting & Vision Assessment

**Purpose:** Clarify client objectives and create measurable success criteria.

#### SMART-ER Goals Framework (UK Context)

**Specific:** What exactly do you want to achieve?
- Professional goals
- Personal development goals
- Relationship goals
- Health and wellbeing goals

**Measurable:** How will you know you've succeeded?
- Key performance indicators
- Milestone markers
- Progress metrics

**Achievable:** Is this realistic given your current situation?
- Resources available
- Support systems
- Potential obstacles

**Relevant:** Why is this important to you now?
- Alignment with values
- Impact on life/career
- Urgency factors

**Time-bound:** When do you want to achieve this?
- Short-term milestones (3 months)
- Medium-term goals (6 months)
- Long-term vision (12+ months)

**Exciting:** What makes this goal inspiring?
**Reviewed:** How often will we review progress?

### 7. Personality & Behavioural Assessment

**Purpose:** Understand client's personality traits and behavioural patterns.

#### Quick DISC Assessment

**Dominant (D)**
- Direct and results-oriented
- Likes challenges and quick decisions
- Values: Results, action, power

**Influential (I)**
- Enthusiastic and optimistic
- Enjoys collaboration and recognition
- Values: Recognition, relationships, fun

**Steady (S)**
- Patient and supportive
- Prefers stability and teamwork
- Values: Security, harmony, helping

**Conscientious (C)**
- Analytical and detail-oriented
- Likes accuracy and quality
- Values: Accuracy, expertise, quality

### 8. Session Feedback Form

**Purpose:** Gather ongoing feedback to improve coaching effectiveness.

#### Post-Session Review:

**Today's Session (Date: _______)**

1. **Key Insights:** What was your biggest takeaway?
2. **Action Items:** What will you commit to before our next session?
3. **Progress Rating:** How would you rate today's session? (1-10)
4. **Support Needed:** What additional support would help?
5. **Topics for Next Time:** What would you like to explore next?

**Coach Effectiveness:**
- Did you feel heard and understood?
- Was the session well-structured?
- Did you receive valuable insights?
- Are you clear on next steps?

4. Are you currently seeing a therapist or counsellor?
5. Any other health considerations I should be aware of?

**Section 6: Legal & Ethical**

- [ ] I understand the difference between coaching and therapy
- [ ] I agree to maintain confidentiality about other clients
- [ ] I understand the coach's confidentiality obligations
- [ ] I agree to the cancellation policy (24 hours notice)
- [ ] I consent to sessions being recorded (if applicable)
- [ ] I understand my data rights under UK GDPR

## üîí GDPR Compliance Checklist

### Essential Elements for UK Coaches:

**Lawful Basis for Processing**
- [ ] Consent obtained and documented
- [ ] Legitimate interests assessment completed
- [ ] Contract for services in place

**Transparency Requirements**
- [ ] Privacy notice provided
- [ ] Data retention periods specified
- [ ] Third-party sharing disclosed
- [ ] Client rights explained

**Data Subject Rights**
- [ ] Right to access procedures
- [ ] Right to erasure process
- [ ] Right to portability enabled
- [ ] Complaint procedure (ICO details)

**Security Measures**
- [ ] Encrypted storage for digital forms
- [ ] Locked filing for paper forms
- [ ] Password protection on all devices
- [ ] Secure communication channels
