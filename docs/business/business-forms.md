Comprehensive collection of professional business forms, contracts, and legal documents specifically designed for UK coaching practices, ensuring compliance with UK law and professional standards.

## üìÑ Essential Coaching Contracts

### 1. Professional Coaching Agreement (UK Standard)

**Purpose:** Establish clear terms of engagement between coach and client.

#### Key Contract Sections:

**1. Parties to the Agreement**
```
This Agreement is entered into on [DATE] between:

Coach: [Name], trading as [Business Name]
Registered Address: [Address]
Company Number: [If applicable]

Client: [Name]
Address: [Address]
```

**2. Services Provided**
```
The Coach agrees to provide professional coaching services including:
- One-to-one coaching sessions
- Duration: [60/90 minutes] per session
- Frequency: [Weekly/Fortnightly/Monthly]
- Format: [In-person/Online/Hybrid]
- Location: [Address/Platform]
```

**3. Fees and Payment Terms**
```
Session Rate: ¬£[Amount] per session (plus VAT where applicable)
Package Rate: ¬£[Amount] for [Number] sessions
Payment Terms: 
- Payment due within 7 days of invoice
- Late payment interest: 8% above Bank of England base rate
- Accepted methods: Bank transfer/Card/PayPal
VAT Registration Number: [If applicable]
```

**4. Cancellation Policy**
```
- 24 hours notice required for cancellation/rescheduling
- Less than 24 hours notice: Full session fee charged
- No-show: Full session fee charged
- Coach cancellation: Session rescheduled at client convenience
- Force majeure provisions included
```

**5. Confidentiality Clause**
```
The Coach agrees to maintain strict confidentiality except:
- Where required by law
- Where there is risk of harm to self or others
- Where explicitly agreed in writing
- For professional supervision (anonymised)
```

**6. Limitations and Disclaimers**
```
- Coaching is not therapy, counselling, or medical treatment
- Results cannot be guaranteed
- Client responsible for their own decisions and actions
- Coach maintains professional indemnity insurance
```

**7. Data Protection (UK GDPR)**
```
Personal data will be processed in accordance with:
- UK General Data Protection Regulation
- Data Protection Act 2018
- Full privacy policy available at: [Website/Document]
```

**8. Termination Clause**
```
Either party may terminate with 14 days written notice
Immediate termination permitted for:
- Breach of contract
- Professional misconduct
- Non-payment of fees
```

**9. Governing Law**
```
This Agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales
Disputes subject to exclusive jurisdiction of English courts
```

### 1. VAT Registration Checklist

**When to Register:**
- Turnover exceeds ¬£90,000 in 12 months
- Expected to exceed threshold in 30 days
- Voluntary registration benefits

**Registration Requirements:**
- [ ] Government Gateway account
- [ ] Business bank details
- [ ] Turnover figures

**Flat Rate Scheme (Coaching):**
- Limited cost business: 16.5%
- Other coaching services: 12%
- Simplified accounting
- Cannot reclaim VAT on purchases
