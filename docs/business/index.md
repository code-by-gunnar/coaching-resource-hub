## ğŸ“Š Business Models to Consider

### One-on-One Coaching
**Best for:** Personalized transformation work
- Individual coaching sessions (60-90 minutes)
- Session packages (3, 6, or 12 sessions)
- Monthly retainer arrangements
- VIP intensive days

### Group Coaching Programs
**Best for:** Scaling your impact and income
- Small group coaching (3-8 people)
- Cohort-based programs (8-12 weeks)
- Masterminds and peer coaching circles
- Online group programs with community

### Course + Coaching Hybrid
**Best for:** Passive income with high-touch support
- Self-paced online courses
- Course + group coaching combination
- Membership communities
- Done-with-you programs

### Corporate & Team Coaching
**Best for:** Higher rates and steady income
- Executive coaching contracts
- Team development programs  
- Leadership coaching initiatives
- Organizational change support

## ğŸ’¡ Success Tips from Established Coaches

### Focus on Your Niche
"The riches are in the niches. Don't try to help everyone - specialize and become known for solving specific problems for specific people."

### Invest in Relationships
"Your network is your net worth. Focus on building genuine relationships, not just collecting contacts."

### Track Your Numbers
"What gets measured gets managed. Track your marketing efforts, conversion rates, and client outcomes."

### Keep Learning
"The coaching industry evolves quickly. Stay current with best practices, new techniques, and industry trends."

### Price for Value, Not Time
"Don't sell hours - sell transformations. Focus on the value you provide, not the time you spend."

## âš ï¸ Common Business Mistakes to Avoid

### Starting Without a Plan
- Skipping the business planning phase
- Not defining your ideal client clearly
